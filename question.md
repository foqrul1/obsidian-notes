**RFID** stands for **Radio Frequency Identification**.

ðŸ‘‰ **Simple definition (viva answer):**  
RFID is a technology used to **identify and track objects wirelessly** using radio waves.

### How it works (very simple):

- An **RFID tag** is attached to an object
    
- An **RFID reader** sends radio signals
    
- The tag responds with its information
    
- The reader reads the data **without physical contact**
